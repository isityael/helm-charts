Thank you for installing the Forgejo Actions runner.

1. Ensure the registration token Secret is present unless runner.existingRunnerSecret is set.
2. Set runner.instanceURL to the Forgejo instance URL.
3. Watch the runner pod logs for first registration and daemon startup.

Example:
  kubectl -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} logs deploy/{{ include "forgejo-runner.fullname" . }} -c runner -f
